Frequently Asked Questions
FASTag is an electronic toll collection system in India using RFID technology. A FASTag sticker is affixed to your vehicle's windshield and linked to your prepaid wallet. As you pass through a toll plaza, the toll amount is automatically deducted from your wallet — no stopping required.
Go to Recharge Wallet in the sidebar. You can add funds using UPI, Debit Card, Credit Card, or Net Banking. The amount is credited to your wallet instantly and is ready for use at any National Highway toll plaza.
Yes! You can register multiple vehicles under a single FASTag GI account. Each vehicle will have its own FASTag linked to your shared wallet balance. Go to My Vehicles to add new vehicles.
Ensure your FASTag sticker is properly affixed to the inner side of your windshield (top-centre, behind the rear-view mirror). Check that your wallet has sufficient balance. If the problem persists, contact NHAI helpline at 1033 or email support@ihmcl.co.in.
Recharges via UPI and Debit/Credit Cards are typically instant. Net Banking transactions may take up to 30 minutes. Your wallet balance will update automatically once the payment is confirmed.
If your payment was deducted but the transaction shows as "Failed", the amount will typically be reversed to your original payment method within 5–7 business days. For recharge refunds, contact your bank. For toll-related issues, contact NHAI at 1033.
